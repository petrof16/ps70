<br> #### Week 3: Hand Tools and Fabrication I am making a cowbell playing kinetic sculpture. <img src="../images/platform.jpeg" alt="platform" width="484" height="363"> I screwed a metal rod onto a wooden platform. Next, I cut two wooden square rods to the same length using the band saw. <img src="../images/cowbell.jpeg" alt="cowbell" width="484" height="363"> I then drilled a single hole in each of the wooden sticks so that I may thread a thin metal rod through them that would provide the fulcrum. I put some tape on the thin metal rod between the two wooden, square sticks so that they would remain the correct length apart. <img src="../images/metal.jpeg" alt="motor" width="484" height="363"> In order to screw the motor onto the wooden platform, I drilled two holes onto a thin metal sheet and bent it so that the motor would reside between the cowbell and the fulcrum. Inspired by Leonardo da Vinci's Power hammer, I modelled two small cams using fusion 360. <img src="../images/davinci.jpeg" alt="davinci" width="640" height="640"> One would have a horizontal hole for the motor arm, and the other, a vertical hole to fit over the motor arm. <img src="../images/horizontal.png" alt="horizontal" width="349" height="269"> <img src="../images/vertical.png" alt="vertical" width="349" height="269"> <img src="../images/cams.jpeg" alt="cams" width="484" height="363"> Six equal-height feet were glued onto the bottom of the platform to provide space for the screws extruding fromt the bottom. I assume it will also help with the sound production. <img src="../images/blocks.jpeg" alt="blocks" width="484" height="363"> I then realized that the cardboard cams were too flimsy, so I used the laser cutter to make the cams out of wood. This made their revolutions much more consistent and stable. In order to secure the sticks in place, I bent two more thin metal rods: <img src="../images/woodcams.jpeg" alt="wood cams" width="484" height="363"> <video src="../images/cowbellvid.mp4" controls> </video> The final step was to write an Arduino code to use a servo motor, which I physically added to the platform.
