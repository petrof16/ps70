<br> #### Week 3: Hand Tools and Fabrication I am making a cowbell playing kinetic sculpture. <img src="../images/cowbell.jpg" alt="cowbell" width="4032" height="3024"> I screwed a metal rod onto a wooden platform. Next, I cut two wooden square rods to the same length using the band saw. I then drilled a single hole in each of the wooden sticks so that I may thread a thin metal rod through them that would provide the fulcrum.
