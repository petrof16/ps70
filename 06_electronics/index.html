<br> #### Week 6: Electronic Input Devices Because it is difficult to be able to practice my piano during the day (due to my toddler son insisting that I either stop and play with him, or that he be the one playing the piano), I recently had the QuietTime ProRecord module added to my acoustic grand piano. Through a series of infra red leds tucked underneath the keybed, it is possible to read the velocity of the key strikes. From these data, MIDI (musical instrument digital interface) messages can be sent out. I used the arduino uno to be able receive the input from the MIDI out messages that get sent out from the piano.
