Using the fender rhodes sustain pedal as a starting off point. <div class="col-sm-4"> ![fender rhodes pedal](./images/rhodespedal.jpg) </div> 2. Charge your phone by converting the kinetic energy from playing the piano into electricity. 3. Create an "arm" that attaches to the piano bench where a laptop, effects pedals, typing keyboard, ect... can be easily be placed. The "arm" can be moved and locked to varying locations so that the pianist can quickly put it in front of them or to the side during a performance. ![inside a piano](./images/insidepiano.jpg) ![piano pedals](./images/pianopedals.jpg)
